ABOUT EVENT

ByteSync 2025
The Ultimate Technical Symposium

BYTESYNC 2025, hosted by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Periyar Maniammai Institute of Science & Technology, on April 4, 2025, is a dynamic Technical Symposium bringing together innovators, problem solvers, and creative minds from across the country. The event features a range of technical and non-technical competitions, providing participants with the opportunity to showcase their skills, challenge their intellect, and engage in thrilling contests. Whether you're a coding enthusiast, designer, or tech lover, BYTESYNC 2025 is the perfect platform to compete, collaborate, and push the boundaries of innovation. Dont miss this opportunity to be part of an unforgettable experience!
